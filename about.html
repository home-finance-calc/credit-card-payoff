How It Works

Our calculator uses the standard compound interest formula to determine your payoff timeline. When you enter your current balance, annual percentage rate (APR), and desired monthly payment, we calculate:

  • How many months until you're debt-free
  • Total interest you'll pay over the life of the debt
  • Total amount paid (principal + interest)
  • Comparison to minimum payment scenarios

This helps you see the real cost of your debt and motivates you to pay more than the minimum whenever possible.
